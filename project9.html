superman

Superman, born Kal-El on Krypton, debuted in 1938 (Action Comics #1) as the first superhero, known for flight, strength, heat/X-ray vision, and weakness to Kryptonite, inspired by figures like Moses, while his secret identity, Clark Kent, mimics silent film actor Harold Lloyd, showcasing his powers under Earth's yellow sun, despite being an alien from Krypton sent to Earth.
